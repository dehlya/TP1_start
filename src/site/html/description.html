The invoked One

By group D

This game is our own take of The binding of Isaac meets Darl souls . The goal of the main character is to bring back peace to the world after one the Primordial god purged the world of humans and destroyed its brothers and sisters. Only a few of them are left, including “Faith”, who invokes our main character to try and bring back hope to humanity.

Game description

A battle against primordial gods. One in particular, Despair, is set on destroying his brothers and sisters in an attempt to govern the living world and be all powerfull. Faith is humanity's last hope, she invokes our main caracter, who is a lost hero from the past, to try and save humanity.

Goal of the game

The character is a hero from the past who has memories of his previous life and an urge to help others in times of need. Despite his weakness after being resurrected at the start of the game, he is going to gain power by killing monsters during his journey, until he fights the god Despair as the final boss.

Character

The character must fight monsters that were brought to earth by the Primordial god Despair. You gain faith points as you kill enemies. The more you kill, the stronger you get. You can use this faith to upgrade your levels and therefore gain power to kill greater enemies.

Rule of the game

The rules of the game are:

  • You must defeat enemies to upgrade your abilities and move forward in the game

  • You lose health points by getting hit by the enemies

  • You lose all your faith by getting killed

  • Each time you rest, the enemies respawn (excluding minor bosses)

  • You must defeat level bosses to move forward in another levels

  • You must defeat the final boss to complete the game
