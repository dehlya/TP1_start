Game process (The invoked one)

The map

In this part we will describe the core of our game, what objects compose the game, how they interact with each other and what they can do. This game is a 2D maze where the player evolves in a map composed by multiple frames in a 8x8 square

Our map will be contained in this array (not every spot will contain a part of the map)

Each frame will have the same background but different layers of decoration. It will have a base size of (1280 x 960) They will have possibly 4 exits. For example if the square on the left contains a part of the map there will be an exit in the right side of the frame, if it don’t there will be no exit on the right side.

The main character

Equipement

Now we will talk about the main character. Our main character will have equipement and an inventory. The equipement will be composed by 6 pieces of equipement (The gloves, the boots, the chest, the helmet, the main hand, and the second hand). Each space can be filles by a corresponding piece of equipement or it can be empty. Our hero will have is own inventory to manage is equipements. It will also have basic stats that can be upgraded by leveling up. The stats will influe over how much damage he will inflict to ennemies, how much life he will possess, the damage taken by ennemy hits, etc…

He will have an hitbox to interact with ennemies attack (loose hp)

Movement

Our hero can be moved in 8 different direction :

He will also have the ability to dash in on of those directions. The dash will allow our character to move quickly in one direction and to gain an invincibilty state for a second.

States

Our main character will have several states.

Idle state:

It’s the basic state when our hero don’t move.

Moving state: It’s the state that our charachter have when he is moving

Invicible state:

This state will be granted to our character a short amount of time will dashing or after being hit.

Attack state:

This state is the state that our character as when he attacks.

Blocking state:

This state is used when our character as a shield and blocks.

Dead state:

This state is used when the HP of our character reaches 0.

Statistics:

Health points :

As long as this stat is greater than 0 our charachter will be alive. He can loose health points by getting hit by an ennemy, and increase health points by drinking a healing potion or interacting with a rest place (represented by a room with a fire). The amount of health points can be increased by adding points in “Vigor”

Stamina:

Stamina is used by our character to do some specific actions. When you attack, when you dash and when you block. When your stamina reaches 0 you can’t do any of the previous actions. To retrieve stamina points you have to wait a short amount of time without using any actions. We will then start to retrieve our stamina back over time. if you use an action during retrieving your stamina this will interrupt the process. The amount of stamina points can be increased by adding points in “Constitution”.

Strength: Your Strength can be increased 1 point each time you gain a level. This will add some damages at your weapon depending on his attack power.

Dexterity: Your Dexterity can be increased 1 point each time you gain a level. This will allow your character to move faster and it will increase the distance of your dash.

Vigor:

Your Vigor can be increased 1 point each time you gain a level. Each point allowed in Vigor will increase the number of Health points of your charachter.

Constitution: Your Constitution can be increased 1 point each time you gain a level.Each point allowed in Constitution will increase the number of Stamina points of your charachter.

Armor:

Your Armor is defined by your equipement. It will block ennemy damages by a % of their attack power.

Attack power:

The Attack power is defined by your weapon and you Strength. This is the damage you will inflict on your ennemies reduced by their own armor.

Faith:

This is the amount of faith you have. You will have to pay an amount of faith to level up depending of your current level.

Level:

This is the level of you character it will define the number of Faith you will need to get to the next level. When you level up you can allow one point in strength, dexterity, vigor or constitution.

Equipements

Main Hand

The main hand is your main weapon (for example: sword, axe, spear, etc…). It will have a unique statistic : attack power. Each weapons will have an hitbox to interact with ennemies.

If you have no equipement on this slot you will punch with your fist. Base attack power of your fists is defined when you create you character. Your weapon will define how much damage you can do.

Second Hand

The second hand is a shield. It will allow you to block damages by consuming your stamina. It will have a unique statistic : block. This stat can go from 0 to 100 and will define the % of damages blocked. The highest the block is the more it will consume your stamina.

Chest, Gloves, Boots, Helmet

This equipements have a unique statistic : armor. They will add armor to your charachter and help him block a part of the damages.

Health potion

Your charachter will have an amount of health potion that can be used to regenerate your health points.

Ennemies

The ennemies will be generated in each room and will have different levels of strength. Each ennemy as 3 main statistics : attack power, health points and armor. Depending on his strength it will give you a certain amount of “Faith”. Ennemies will have an hitbox. If their hitbox interact with the hitbox of your weapon they will loose hp.

Boss

The bosses are like standard ennemies but they will be bigger and stronger.

Rest place

This is a place that you can encounter in some rooms and that will allow you to regenerate your health points, refill your heealth potions and level up. When you interact with a rest place (represented by a fire) every ennemies on the map will respawn.
